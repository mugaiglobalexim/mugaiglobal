About Us

Your global partner in electronic supply and design.

Mugai Global is a reliable supplier and distributor of Electronic Components, Connectors, Electro Mechanical products, Wires & Cables along with diverse range of Motors, Pumps, Gearboxes, Machined components and both Semi-assembled and Fully Assembled PCBAs.

In addition, We specialise in designing and developing Electronic products from concept stage to Production which includes Concept formulation, Specification determination, Design, Prototype construction, Testing and Product manufacturing.
